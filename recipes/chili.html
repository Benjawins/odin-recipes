Chili

A healthy, fast, and cheap chili.

Ingredients

  • 400g Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1tsp Onion powder
  • 1tsp Garlid powder
  • 1tsp Smoked paprika powder

Steps

  1. Add onion in large soup pot, cook 5 minutes.
  2. Add ground beef and break it apart, cook for 6-7 minutes.
  3. Add chili, and all other seasonings, add diced tomatoes
  4. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, let rest 5-10 minutes before serving.
