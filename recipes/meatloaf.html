Meatloaf

The finished meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 400g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 250ml milk
  • Salt and pepper and other seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 175 degrees C
  2. Mix all ingredients, place in lightly greased loaf pan
  3. Bake at 175 degrees C for 1 hour.
