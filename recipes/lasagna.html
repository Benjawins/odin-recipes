Lasagna

A healthy, fast, and cheap lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Lasagna noodles
  • 400g Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 200g shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 box ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water, add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 min
  2. Preheat oven 175 degrees C, brown beef and season, drain, then stir in tomato sauce garlic and simmer 5 min
  3. Combine all the cheeses, stir well. Alternate layers of noodles, meat and cheese
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 min, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
